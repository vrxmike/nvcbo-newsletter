GOTU VILLAGE: IMPACT STORY

"Light in the Darkness"

Families in Gotu now have safer, smoke-free light after dark, improving study conditions, household safety, and evening productivity. Building on thorough community-led research to clearly identify needs and priorities, Northern Vision CBO distributed 300 solar lanterns in Gotu (Sands of Gold community), replacing hazardous kerosene lamps with clean, renewable energy solutions.
