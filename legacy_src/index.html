Who We Are

Driving Sustainable Change

Founded in 2020, NorthernVision CBO focuses on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 5, 10, and 15. We place special emphasis on empowering smallholder farmers, particularly women and youth, through climate resilience, digital skills, and resource management.
