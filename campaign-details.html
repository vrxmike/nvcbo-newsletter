Light in the Darkness

The Gotu Initiative: Bringing solar power to the "Sons of Gold"

In the pastoralist community of Gotu, Dahaba, sunset often meant the end of productivity. Without access to the national grid, students struggled to study, and women faced security challenges in the dark. Last month, Northern Vision CBO changed that narrative.

Mission: More Than Just Light

Our mission was simple but profound: ensure that every home in Gotu has access to light. We believe access to clean energy is not a giftâ€”it is justice. Every family deserves the power to learn, live, and be safe under their own light.

"Each lantern brings connection, security, dignity, and hope into their homes. A small start, but a powerful light for our community's future."

The Impact in Numbers

  • 300 Solar Lanterns distributed to off-grid families.
  • 150+ Households now have sustainable night lighting.
  • 3 Key Groups supported: Students, Women, and Herders.
