Our Path to Resilience: A Year of Survival and Dignity

ZB

Zamzam Bonaya Boru

Executive Director, Northern Vision CBO

Northern Vision CBO is more than a grassroots organization; it is a movement building a just and sustainable future for pastoralist communities. Our path in women’s land rights is not just about equity; it’s about survival, dignity, and the future of our people.

As we close 2025, we look back at milestones that highlight our commitment to local action with global reach. This year, we moved from indigenous healing circles in Northern Kenya to the global climate negotiations at COP30. We are proving that collective action can overcome systemic obstacles.

"The Land Is Our Mother"

We launched ‘Laaf Haadha Teenna’, a groundbreaking initiative using cultural healing to address trauma and empower women to claim their land rights. By shifting voice and agency to grassroots women, we are changing the very landscape of Kenyan land reform.
